Mission

I (MisterTicot) created Cosmic.Plus with the following goals in mind:

  • Providing end-users with some fundamental high-quality softwares.
  • Providing an high-level open source development kit for Stellar applications.
  • Defending the interests of the community by promoting balanced & vendor-neutral solutions for the technical challenges we're facing.
  • Coordinating with other developers that are making efforts in the same direction.

Philosophy

Cosmic.Plus embraces the cooperative mindset of the free software community. I believe that in a competitive space, it is critically important that some independent actors are able to contribute without having their private interest directly at stake.

Each Cosmic.Plus software get released under a permissive open source licence (MIT). What is made publicly available doesn't need to be coded time and time again. This enables my fellow developers to focus on what makes their applications unique, which ultimately benefit to end users.

Important design choices are publicly discussed with the community prior to implementation, and feedback is systematically taken into account.

Economic Model

Naturally, achieving those goals comes at a cost. However, it would not be possible to "play it fair" while relying on aggressive monetization strategies. Hence, this project runs a non-profit, ethical economic model.

So far, I financed it thanks to Build Challenges the Stellar Development Foundation organized. In the future, I'll try to finance the hard work from other kinds of contest and from donations.

I'm conscious that maintaining such standard is a demanding challenge. I'm confident that Cosmic.Plus can reach economic stability thanks to community & SDF support.

Privacy

Privacy is a right. Cosmic.Plus pieces applications don't use ads/trackers and don't collect/publish data about users activity. However, Cosmic.Plus web applications are distributed through GitHub, Cloudflare and to a lesser extend Azure. Those services may keep logs about who is using the apps.
